Game Rules

With magnifying glasses in hand, players take turn looking for the ladybugs with the most spots as possible. The more spots a ladybug has, the rarer it is and the more points you earn! After each search, players can choose to test their luck looking for more or take a break and let their opponents to have a go to keep their score safe, But be careful—hidden among the ladybugs are pesky critters!

If you stumble upon one, your turn ends and the other player gets a chance to hunt. Disaster strikes if you run into two unwanted critters in a single round! All your hard-earned points scurry away, leaving you back at zero. Guess the bugs weren’t on your side this time!

The first player to reach 30 points or more wins and is declared as the Ladybug Whisperer!
Ready, set, go!
